Jorge Esparteiro Garcia

Assistant Professor at the Instituto Politécnico de Viana do Castelo (IPVC). Director of ADiT-Lab and Affiliated Researcher at INESC TEC.

Instituto Politécnico de Viana do Castelo (IPVC)

Viana do Castelo, Portugal

Email: jorgegarcia@esce.ipvc.pt

GitHub · LinkedIn

He holds a PhD in Informatics Engineering from the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (FEUP), with a thesis entitled "Requirements Change Management based on Web Usage Mining". He also holds a master's degree in Informatics Engineering from FEUP and a degree in Computer Science from the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Porto (FCUP).

Over his academic career, he has authored more than 50 publications, including books, book chapters, journal articles, and conference proceedings. He has supervised over 35 master's students, as well as PhD and postdoctoral researchers in areas related to software engineering and digital transformation.

He has participated in more than 15 funded research and innovation projects at both national and international levels, reinforcing his strong commitment to applied research and knowledge transfer.
